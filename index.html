Frequently Asked Questions

What types of AI/ML projects do you work on?

I work on a wide range of AI/ML projects including predictive modeling, computer vision applications, natural language processing, recommendation systems, and custom machine learning solutions tailored to specific business needs. Whether it's developing models from scratch or optimizing existing systems, I can help bring your AI vision to life.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. A simple ML model might take 2-3 weeks, while complex systems can take 2-3 months or more. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific project scope.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

I work with clients of all sizes! From startups looking to implement their first AI solution to established companies scaling existing systems. I believe in making AI accessible and can tailor my approach to fit different budgets and requirements.

What is your development process?

My process includes: 1) Initial consultation and requirements gathering, 2) Data exploration and preprocessing, 3) Model development and training, 4) Testing and validation, 5) Deployment and integration, 6) Documentation and training, 7) Ongoing support and maintenance. I maintain clear communication throughout and provide regular updates.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! I offer various support packages including bug fixes, model retraining, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. I believe in building long-term relationships and ensuring your AI solutions continue to perform optimally.

Can you help with existing AI/ML systems?

Absolutely! I can review, optimize, and enhance existing AI/ML systems. This includes improving model accuracy, optimizing performance, refactoring code, updating to newer frameworks, and fixing bugs. I also provide code audits and recommendations for improvement.
